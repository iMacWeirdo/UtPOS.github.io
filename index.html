Everything Your Operation Needs

Order Management

Take dine-in, takeout, and delivery orders from a fast, intuitive interface built for busy service environments.

Payment Processing

Accept cash, card, and split payments. Receipt printing and end-of-day reconciliation built in.

Menu & Pricing

Manage your full menu with modifiers, categories, pricing tiers, and daily specials — all editable on the fly.

Reporting & Analytics

Sales summaries, item performance reports, and shift breakdowns — all exportable as CSV.

Open Source (GPLv3)

No licensing fees, no forced upgrades, no vendor lock-in. The software is yours to run, modify, and extend.

Lightweight & Fast

Low hardware requirements and a minimal footprint mean UtPOS runs reliably even on older terminal equipment.

Kitchen Display System Support

UtPOS routes orders directly to your kitchen screens — including legacy Oasys hardware you may already own.

Oasys IPadSX Compatibility

Our protocol engine was developed through clean-room Wireshark analysis of the ChefXML/ROD Gold communication layer, enabling full compatibility with your existing Oasys IPadSX displays. No hardware replacement needed.

Real-Time Order Routing

New tickets appear on the KDS the moment they are submitted at the terminal. Bump and recall controls let kitchen staff manage order flow efficiently.

Multi-Screen Support

Route different item categories (grill, prep, expo) to separate screens. Configurable station layouts adapt to your kitchen workflow.

Reduce E-Waste

End-of-life ChefXML/ROD Gold software doesn't have to mean end-of-life hardware. UtPOS gives your Oasys screens a second life and keeps working equipment out of the landfill.
